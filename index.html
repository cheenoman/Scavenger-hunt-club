About Us

The Tasting Trailblazers is a community of food enthusiasts passionate about exploring diverse cuisines and cultures through engaging scavenger hunts. Our mission is to celebrate cultures, support small businesses, and create lasting relationships.

Activities

  • Taste Test Treasure: A culinary scavenger hunt.
  • Around the World in a Day: Explore cuisines from multiple cultures in one event.
  • Cooking + Clueing: Interactive cooking sessions with clues.

Membership Benefits

  • Cultural exchange and exploration
  • Exclusive discounts
  • Invitations to Michelin-starred restaurants
